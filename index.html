What We Do

24/7 AI-Powered Conversations

Julie-Anne provides constant support through advanced AI-powered conversations, offering a safe and non-judgmental space to share thoughts, seek guidance, and find solace. Available day or night, it's here when you need it most, with a friendly and understanding tone.

Reducing the Burden on Services

By providing immediate support, Julie-Anne helps to alleviate the pressure on traditional mental health services like the NHS. It offers a first point of contact for those who might otherwise face long waiting times, guiding users to professional help when needed.

Global Reach, Local Impact

Starting in the UK, Julie-Anne aims to reach communities worldwide, bringing empathetic support and connection to those who feel isolated. It is designed to complement existing local services and provide immediate, compassionate support.

Immediate Support Resources

If you or someone you know is in crisis, please reach out to the following organizations for immediate help:

  • Samaritans - Free 24/7 Support - Call: 116 123
  • Mind - Mental Health Charity - Call: 0300 123 3393
  • NHS Mental Health Services - Call: 111
  • PAPYRUS - Prevention of Young Suicide - Call HOPELINEUK: 0800 068 4141
  • CALM - Campaign Against Living Miserably - Call: 0800 58 58 58

The Heart of Julie-Anne

Julie-Anne was created in memory of my nan, Julie-Anne Fitzgerald, who never judged, always listened, and helped me through some of my darkest times. Her warmth, compassion, and unwavering support inspired me to develop a service that could do the same for others. This platform is a tribute to her legacy—a way to offer hope and comfort to those who feel alone, just as she did for me. Our mission is to reach those who need a kind word, a listening ear, or a reminder that they are not alone.
